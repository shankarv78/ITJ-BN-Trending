# Part 2: The Holy Grail Formula (AWLR)

## Adjusted Win/Loss Ratio

```
AWLR = (Average Gain Ã— % Winning Trades) / (Average Loss Ã— % Losing Trades)
```

### Examples:
- **Example 1:** (10% Ã— 50%) / (5% Ã— 50%) = **2:1 AWLR**
- **Example 2:** (15% Ã— 40%) / (5% Ã— 60%) = **2:1 AWLR**

### The Trading Triangle
Three variables you can adjust:
1. **Average Gain** - Size of your winners
2. **Average Loss** - Size of your losers (most controllable!)
3. **Batting Average** - Percentage of winning trades

**Critical Insight:** You can compensate for lower batting average by improving your win/loss ratio.

# Part 5: Progressive Exposure System

## The Scale-Up/Scale-Down Framework

### When Starting (Pilot Buys)
1. Start with **quarter position** (6.25%)
2. DO NOT get aggressive until trades start working
3. Use profits to finance increased risk

### Progressive Position Ladder

| Trade # | Outcome | Position Size | Risk ($100K account) |
|---------|---------|---------------|---------------------|
| 1 | WIN | 6.25% (Quarter) | $500 |
| 2 | WIN | 12.5% (Half) | $1,000 |
| 3 | WIN | 25% (Full) | $2,000 |
| 4 | LOSS | 25% (Full) | ($2,000) |
| 5 | LOSS | 12.5% (Half) | ($1,000) |

**Golden Rule:** "Trade the largest when you're trading your best... and trade the smallest when you're trading your worst."

## 2:1 Reward/Risk Financing Rule

At 2:1 R/R, every win finances TWO losses:

| Trade | Stop Risk | Outcome | P&L | Running P&L |
|-------|-----------|---------|-----|-------------|
| 1 | $1,000 | Win (2R) | +$2,000 | +$2,000 |
| 2 | $1,000 | Loss | -$1,000 | +$1,000 |
| 3 | $1,000 | Loss | -$1,000 | **$0 (Breakeven)** |

# Part 6: Stop Loss Strategy

## Two Types of Stops

| Technical Stop | Mathematical Stop |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Based on chart structure | Based on % from entry |
| Support levels, patterns | Fixed 5%, 6%, 8% etc. |
| Where price "shouldn't go" | Risk/reward calculation |

**Best Practice:** Back into technical stops that make mathematical sense.

## Stop Loss Guidelines

| Stop Type | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| Very Tight | 3% or less |
| Average | 4-6% |
| Maximum | 7-8% |
| Uncle Point (staggered only) | 10-12% |

## Staggered/Bracketed Stops

| Half Position | Half Position | Average |
|---------------|---------------|---------|
| 6% stop | 10% stop | 8% average |
| 4% stop | 12% stop | 8% average |

# Part 10: 14 Essential Trading Rules

1. **Always trade with a stop-loss** - Know exit BEFORE entry
2. **Never risk more than you can expect to gain** on average
3. **NEVER average down** - "Only losers average losers"
4. **Scale up when trading well, scale down when trading poorly**
5. **Only add to positions in the direction of the trade**
6. **Cut overweight portion immediately** if trade moves against you
7. **Trim excess position** if stock fails to move as expected
8. **Never hold large position into major report** (earnings)
9. **Never let a good size gain turn into a loss**
10. **Move stop to breakeven ASAP**
11. **Sell immediately on material behavior change**
12. **Look for follow-up buying after breakout**
13. **Don't have favorites** - Buy in order of breakout
14. **Evaluate position daily** - Always think risk vs. reward
